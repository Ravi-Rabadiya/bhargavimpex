Quality Inspections

Inspections involve examining products or services at different stages of production or delivery to ensure they meet specified criteria. Inspections can be carried out visually or using specialized tools and equipment.

Quality Audits

Audits involve systematic examinations of processes, procedures, or systems to verify compliance with quality standards and identify areas for improvement.

Testing and Sampling

Testing involves subjecting products to various tests to assess their performance, durability, reliability, and other quality attributes. Sampling involves selecting a representative sample of products for testing from a larger batch.

Quality Management Systems (QMS)

QMS frameworks such as ISO 9001 provide a structured approach to quality control by defining processes, procedures, and responsibilities for ensuring quality throughout an organization.
